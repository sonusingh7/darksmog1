About Redifice Bird Of Paradise

True luxury living is not only about owning the best - it is also about indulging yourself in the finest of experiences within the comfort of your home. Nestled in the heart of the city - MG Road, Millers Road, and Palace Road, Sweet Brooke is where luxury transcends itself into true luxury.

A five-floored, classical-contemporary designed structure, home to 40 luxury apartments. Each unit houses an outdoor deck that is ideal for the peaceful privacy of its esteemed dwellers.

Well connected through a 10 minute’s drive to the major hubs of the city such as the Cantonment Railway Station, Indiranagar and MG Road, step into a home built on rich heritage, with inspiration of the old city instilled into the new.

Specifications

1. Structure

  • Shell: A sturdy RCC frame structure with columnsand beams, along with masonry partitions.
  • Masonry: Top grade lightweight concrete block masonry with plaster.

2. Flooring & Dado

  • Lobbies & Staircase: Marble or Granite laid with paper joints and finished with 8 coats polish.
  • Living & Dining: Large format iMarble tiles with high rating of strength, durability and no maintenance compared to marble.
  • Kitchen: iMarble tiles for flooring. Granite counters with glazed tiles above the counter iMarble tiles up to a height of 2’.
  • Wet Kitchen: Provided there is enough space, antiskid tiles flooring with glazed tiles above the granite counter up to a height of 2’, will be provided.
  • Utility: Antiskid tiles flooring with wall tiles up to 3’.
  • All Bedrooms: iMarble tiles with high rating of strength, durability and no maintenance compared to marble.
  • Master Bathroom: Marble or Granite or iMarble tile for counters. iMarble tiles for flooring and walls up to 8’.

3. Doors & Windows

  • Frames & Shutters: Finger jointed Ghana Teak or equivalent hardwood will be used for doorframes.
  • Solid core flush (block board shutters provided with a natural wood veneer) will go in the making of door shutters. All main doors are 8’ tall and 4’ wide, bedroom doors are 3’3” wide and bathroom doors are 2’9” wide.
  • First quality UPVC sliding sections 8’height and 6’ to 7’ wide for all windows.
  • UPVC frame and shutter will be used for all bathroom windows, including maid’s room.
  • Mosquito Screens: All regular windows and French Windows will have mosquito screens.
  • Window Grills: French Windows will not have window grills. Grills will only be provided for regular windows where there is a sheer drop. Bathroom windows will also not have grills.
  • Finish: Melamine matt finish or PU (Polyurethane) finish will be provided for internal door shutters and frames.
  • Hardware: Brush-finished stainless steel effect will be given to all hardware. A night latch and a door eye viewer will be offered on the main door.
  • The remaining doors will be provided with door bolts and a mortise lock or tubular / cylindrical lock.

4. Sanitary Fixtures

  • Water Closets: All bathrooms will come with western style, porcelain EWC of Queo or equivalent make, except the Maid’s bathroom, which shall be provided with an EWC of Parryware or equivalent make.
  • Health Faucets: All bathrooms will have health faucets of Jaquar or equivalent make.
  • Wash Basins: All bathrooms will have porcelain washbasins of Queo or equivalent make. The maid’s bathroom will be fitted with Parryware or equivalent make washbasin.
  • Kitchen Sink: Stainless steel sink with drain board.
  • Cockroach Traps: All bathrooms will come with a detachable stainless steel cockroach trap with lid of Queo or equivalent make.
  • Bathtubs: Provided there is enough space, the master bathroom will be provided with a Bathtub of Redifice or equivalent make.
  • Showers: All bathrooms will be fitted with showers of Queo or equivalent make.
  • Faucets: All faucets shall be CP, heavy body metal fittings of Queo or equivalent make.
  • Hot water connection shall be provided to the shower and washbasin in each bathroom and to the sink in the kitchen.
  • Washing Machine Point: Utility space will be provided with one cold water inlet and drainage outlet for a washing machine.
  • Plumbing: All water supply lines and drainage lines shall be in CPVC pipes. Sewage lines shall be in CPVC.

5. Electrical

  • Wiring: All wiring shall be of Polycab or equivalent make, concealed in 19 mm dia, 2 mm thick PVC conduit pipes.
  • Switches: All switches shall be of Norisys or equivalent make.
  • Apart from the basic light and fan controls, the following number of sockets shall be provided in various rooms.
  • Living / Dining: 4 nos - 5 amp switch and socket.
  • Kitchen: 3 nos - 5 amp switch and socket, 3 nos – 15 amp switch and socket.
  • Wet kitchen (T35 only): 3 nos - 5 amp switch and socket, 3 nos - 15 amp switch and socket.
  • Utility: 1 no. - 5 amp switch and socket, 1 no. – 15 amp switch and socket.
  • Master Bedroom: 4 nos - 5 amp switch and socket.

6. Paint

  • Paint: Acrylic Distemper will be used for all internal paint. Exposed tiles, brick or stone masonry will not come with external paint.
  • Cement based paint shall be used for remaining areas. Zinc chromate primer and synthetic enamel paint will be applied to metal railings.
  • Lifts: Two, one 15-passenger capacity, fully automatic elevator with customized luxury interiors & one 15 passenger service elevator.
  • Solar Hot Water System: Solar hot water system along with heat pump.
  • Home Automation: Home Automation for the complete flat.
  • Security System: Video Door Phone system will be provided connecting each flat to the Security Room and connected to Home Automation.
  • Security Cameras on the periphery.
  • BMS (Building Management System) will be implemented for large complex.
  • Main door: Biometric lock for main door and connected to Home Automation.
  • Fire Fighting: Complete fire-fighting system. Smoke detector in every kitchen. Handheld fire extinguishers. Gas leak detector in every kitchen. Piped gas cut off point.
  • Flase Ceiling: It provides easy accessibility for servicing and enhances the visual appeal of home and the bathrooms and keeps service ducts concealed,Is provided for the complete flat except balconies and maid’s and toilet.
  • Cooking Gas: Reticulated piped gas connection to each kitchen, with separate metering system.

7. Landscaping

  • Soil: The base layer for landscaping will have good quality red soil and sand mixture with manure.
  • Irrigation: Landscaped areas shall be provided with sprinklers and a drip irrigation system.
  • Soft Landscaping: All garden areas shall be completely landscaped. Grass, shrubs, bamboo, etc., shall be provided, as well.
  • Water Body: As part of the entrance garden with underwater lights a water body (not for swimming) including a filtration unit shall be provided.
  • Parking Area: Charging points for electric cars & water points for washing vehicles with drain system.
